Meet Barr Miracle Oluchi Ogba

Miracle Oluchi Ogba is a highly accomplished legal professional with a strong commitment to human rights and a proven ability to excel in diverse academic and professional settings. Her academic journey culminated in a Master of Laws (LLM) degree with distinction from Queen Mary University of London, further solidifying her expertise in legal studies. This degree followed a distinguished Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) degree, also earning first-class honors, from Abia State University. Complementing her academic achievements, Ogba completed the Barrister-at-Law (BL) program at the prestigious Nigerian Law School, again attaining first-class honors, which led to her licensure as a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria. ... Ogba’s professional experience reflects her dedication and adaptability. Since 2020, she has served as an Assistant Lecturer at Abia State University, significantly contributing to the legal education of undergraduates and actively participating in designing and implementing both in-person and virtual curricula. Simultaneously, she has developed extensive practical legal skills, including experience at Legal Unit, University of Nigeria Nsukka and Onah and Onah Associate, gaining experience working within legal teams and in litigation. Her roles involved legal research and drafting, client interviewing, representing organizations in court, and providing legal opinions on diverse issues. This demonstrably multifaceted experience allowed her to develop a diverse skillset, including legal writing, case analysis, advocacy, and effective communication.
